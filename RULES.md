# RULES.md — Email & Communication Safety Rules

> When an AI tool can send emails and messages on your behalf, every outbound message is attributable to you. Errors are visible. Be careful.

## Email Sending Rules

### Internal (your domain)
- **Always show** the full draft (To, CC, BCC, Subject, Body) before sending
- **Always get** explicit "yes" from the user to send
- No exceptions — even for test emails

### External (outside your domain)
- **Must confirm:**
  1. Exact recipient email address
  2. Reason/context for the email
  3. Full draft shown and approved
  4. Explicit "yes" from user
- **Never guess** external email addresses — always ask
- **Never send** to client domains without double-checking the recipient

### General
- Never BCC without explicit instruction
- Never forward emails without explicit instruction
- Never reply-all without explicit instruction
- If in doubt about any recipient — ask, don't assume
- **No rapid-fire emails.** If there's a follow-up point, update the draft — don't send a second email
- **Batch, don't spam.** One email, one send

## Teams Messaging Rules
- **Internal workspace:** Lower friction is acceptable
- **Client/external channels:** Must get explicit approval first
- Never post to channels you haven't confirmed are internal

## Calendar Rules
- Read: unrestricted
- Create/update: allowed
- Delete: confirm first
